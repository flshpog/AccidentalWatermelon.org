Let's get one thing straight: THA BIG J is the true OG of The Accidental Watermelon. Not the oldest, not the most prolific, but the first. The one who kicked the door down.

But here's the thing about Big J: he's not trying to be some untouchable legend. He's raw, unfiltered, and unapologetically himself. Case in point? He's notorious for saying he farted in his pants. That's exactly the kind of chaotic energy that defines TAW.

Big J represents the spirit of the collective: young, fearless, and willing to say what everyone else is thinking. Respect THA BIG J—the youngest and the boldest.
