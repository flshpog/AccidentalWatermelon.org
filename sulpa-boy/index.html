Sulpa Boy

Producer / Former Independent / Beat Maker

Makes beats and delivers vocals with equal precision. The hustler, the collaborator.

Before joining TAW, Sulpa Boy was doing her own thing as an independent artist. Solo grind, solo vision. But when she linked up with TAW, something clicked. Now she's collaborating with digitalestrogen and Playboi Bingi.

Sulpa Boy is a double threat: she makes beats and delivers vocals with equal precision. Whether crafting instrumentals or laying down verses, she brings that DIY energy.

And her taste? Impeccable. Sulpa Boy is a huge fan of Joey Valence & Brae and other miscellaneous twinks who dominate her playlists. She's unapologetic about it.

Named after Soulja Boy, she channels that same entrepreneurial, boundary-pushing energy. The hustler, the collaborator, the multi-talented artist. Respect Sulpa Boy.
